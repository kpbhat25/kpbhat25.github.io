Koushik Bhat

Graduate Student IIT Madras

I am a Research Engineer at Collins Aerospace, working in the Applied Research & Technology (ART) team. My current work focuses on Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), and Large Language Models (LLMs).

I earned my MS (by Research) in Electrical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM), where I conducted research at the Sudha Gopalakrishnan Brain Centre (SGBC). Under the guidance of Prof. Mohanasankar Sivaprakasam and Keerthi Ram, my research focused on Biomedical Image Processing, specifically registration and segmentation.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Engineering from B.M.S. College of Engineering.

I am honored to be part of DHARANI: A 3D Developing Human-brain Atlas Resource to Advance Neuroscience Internationally – Integrated Multimodal Imaging and High-resolution Histology of the second trimester. IITM Brain Centre
